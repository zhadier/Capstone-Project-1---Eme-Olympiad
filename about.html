EME Olympiad 2020

​The three most active committees of NUST College of E&ME: Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (IEEE), Society for Adventure and Sports (SAS) and Society for Promotion of Arts and Literature (SPAL) present EME Olympiad 2020. It is a four-day extravaganza consisting of a plethora of activities ranging from singing to robotics to where ever the boundaries of your mind may lie because we shall push it beyond that. Featuring more than 60 contests, it is the the largest event across the twin cities.
